How to ask a question

  • Provide relavant background information:
    • the goal
    • what language
    • what ide
    • what program
  • Use formal language, like you ask a colleage
  • Check if the question has been asked before
  • Proper layout of question
  • 2-3 Senteces - short but correct information about the issue.
  • Post the error along with the question
  • Before you ask the question, check for the spelling in the title, punctuations
  • respond to feedback, let people know what worked for you
